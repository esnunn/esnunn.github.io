About Me

I recently graduated from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo with my Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics.
I enjoy collaborating with teams, exploring the applications of Mathematics
in the fields of Statistics and Computer Science, and helping others.

Yosemite, May 2016

I love hiking! One of my most memorable hiking/camping experiences was in Yosemite National Park. We woke up at 4AM to get to Glacier Point and view the sun rise over Half Dome. I hope to visit Yosemite again soon. It is truly beauty you cannot get anywhere else.

Taiwan, December 2015

I spent two weeks teaching color guard clinics in Taiwan with the Concord Blue Devils, an independent youth organization based in the Bay Area. We performed in various venues for the Taiwanese government and also ran clinics at local high schools. Here is one of the clinics at a school in Chiayi. To find out more about the Concord Blue Devils, you can visit www.bluedevils.org

Europe, Summer 2015

I spent about two months traveling around Europe performing and running clinics with the Concord Blue Devils International Corps. During our trip, we visited The Netherlands, France, Italy, England, Germany, and Switzerland. The people, food, and experiences were unforgettable. My favorite place was the Netherlands and I hope to go back soon.
